Model populations and applicability
PARS (Persian CVD Risk Score)
Derived from Isfahan Cohort Study, Iran, 2001–2010. 6,500+ adults aged 35–75. Outputs exact 10-year CVD event probability (%). Inputs: age, sex, SBP, cholesterol, WHR, diabetes, smoking, family history.
WHO/ISH EMR-B
WHO risk chart for Eastern Mediterranean sub-region B: Bahrain, Iran, Jordan, Kuwait, Lebanon, Libya, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Syria, Tunisia, UAE. Outputs categorical 10-year risk range. Inputs: age, sex, SBP, cholesterol, diabetes, smoking.
Cholesterol conversion: mg/dL ÷ 38.67 = mmol/L (auto-calculated for WHO/ISH). Note: If both models agree on the risk tier, confidence in the estimate is higher. Discordance should prompt clinical judgment.
